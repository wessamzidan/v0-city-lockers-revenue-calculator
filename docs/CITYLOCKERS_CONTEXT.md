## 7. Business Model

### 7.1 Zero Capex - Zero Opex
Partners receive revenue with NO:
- Installation costs
- Maintenance costs
- Marketing costs
- Operational overhead

CityLockers handles:
- Full installation
- All maintenance
- Marketing & customer acquisition
- 24/7 support

### 7.2 Revenue Share
- Typical range: 15-30% of gross revenue
- Paid to partner monthly
- Real-time tracking via portal

### 7.3 Contract Terms
- Standard: 5 years
- Termination: 6 months notice by either party
